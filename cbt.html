UI/UX Design Project

CBT Mental Health App

A mental health mobile app concept designed around Cognitive Behavioural Therapy principles, with role-specific flows for patients, general users, and CBT users.

01

Patients

Access guided support, mental health resources, and progress-focused features.

02

General Users

Explore wellness tools, self-help content, and simple mental health guidance.

03

CBT Users

Use CBT-based activities, reflection tools, and structured exercise flows.

Process

From requirements to tested prototype.

Research

Analysed requirements and identified the needs of different user groups.

User Flow

Mapped app navigation and separate task flows for each user role.

Wireframes

Created low-fidelity screens to plan layout, structure, and content order.

Prototype

Designed high-fidelity Figma screens and connected them into an app prototype.

Testing

Collected user feedback and refined the interface for better clarity.
