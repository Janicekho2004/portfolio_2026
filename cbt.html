UI/UX & Frontend Case Study

CBT Mental Health Mobile Application

A high-fidelity mobile prototype designed to bridge the gap between patients undergoing Cognitive Behavioral Therapy and clinical therapists. Built with a scalable design system for clearer handoff.

Figma Auto Layout Design System

The Challenge

Mental health tracking apps can feel clinical or disconnected from therapy providers. Patients may lose motivation, while therapists may not have enough structured information to understand user progress.

The Solution

Created a multi-role app concept with calming patient flows, CBT-based activities, and structured therapist-facing screens for identity, education, and carrier verification.

User Flow 01: Patient Onboarding & Tracking

Soft gradients designed to reduce anxiety during emotional documentation.

1. CBT Dashboard

Main hub for CBT tools and daily support.

2. Emotion Entries

Records emotional check-ins and reflections.

3. Behaviour Logging

Helps users document patterns and triggers.

4. Progress Statistics

Shows progress and emotional trends over time.
